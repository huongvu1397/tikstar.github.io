How to find your TikTok username

Follow these simple way to find your username

Your username starts with the @sign

The easiest way to find it is to open your TikTok profile tab.

You will see your username under your profile picture, starting with the character @. See the picture below.

Note: If your username is not set, you can set it by pressing the Edit profile button

You can easily verify if your username is correct by opening the Tiktok website with the following URL: https://www.tiktok.com/@your_username.

Use your real username instead of @your_username
